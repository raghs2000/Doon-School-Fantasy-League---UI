From the team

Welcome to the Doon School Fantasy League!

The concept of Fantasy Leagues on the internet has become a sensation across the world. Taking a cue from its popularity, some like-minded, ambitious Doscos have been working on an innovative project to formulate a domestic fantasy league for the upcoming Inter House Basketball competition. In order to forge this dream into a success, we request the school community to actively engage in this event. It was a month back when we came up with this pioneering idea to develop a league similar to the Fantasy Premier League for our own Inter-House competitions. Most of us must be familiar with the Fantasy Premier League, but for those who are not, it is an online game wherein participants assemble an imaginary team of real life soccer players and score points based on those players' actual statistical performance or their perceived contribution on the playing field. Fantasy football, in recent years, has evolved from a simple recreational activity to a global forum for keen football fans due to exposure via the internet.

Now, you must be thinking how incorporating such an app into our school community would be beneficial for us. The answer is pretty simple. It would help us tabulate and analyse figures and statistics of sporting events in a better and more comprehensive way, and would also create a buzz around the school, leading to increased participation in Inter-House events. The application will encourage Doscos to observe sporting matches more keenly and will cater towards a commendable increase in the number of spectators for various competitions. The statistics acquired from this application will further give an in-depth, detailed reference as to how each house team and player performed in the competition.

This application is solely intended for recreational purposes and does not intend to hurt the sentiments of any individual or house at any cost. For any further queries, please feel free to contact us on this email or personally too.
